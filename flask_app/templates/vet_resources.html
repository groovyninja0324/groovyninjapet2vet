Finance Options for Veterinary Bills:

  • CareCredit is a medical credit card (It does a hard pull on your credit) that allows you to pay for veterinary services as well as other human-based medical bills.
  • Scratchpay does a "soft pull" on your credit and is not a credit card. It is a loan for your veterinary bill that you then pay back over a set time.
  • iCare Financial No credit check needed. BUT your veterinary hospital has to already be signed up for this financial option.

 

Crowdfunding:

  • Waggle is an animal-specific crowdfunding site that allows anyone to pay the veterinarian directly for your pet's treatment.
  • GoFundMe is an online fundraising option to raise the money for your veterinary bills.
